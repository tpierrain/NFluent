New feature(s):
* Check.ThatCode(...): New entry point for checks on code (Action and Func<T>). It supersedes the equivalent Check.That signature.


--------------
Change(s):
* Check.That(Func<T>) and Check.That(Action) are now obsolete.
* Simplification of extensibility
* Renames the HasFieldsEqualToThose() check to HasFieldsWithSameValues() which now support anonymous classes as expected parameter.
* Renames the HasFieldsNotEqualToThose() check to HasNotFieldsWithSameValues().
